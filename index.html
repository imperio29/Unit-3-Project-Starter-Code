The Briefcases!

You were walking home when a homeless man approached you. He says I have two briefcases, would you like to take them?

Great! You accepted his offer! He pulls out two identical gray briefcases. You take them and head home.

That was weird! You're glad that you didnt take anything from a stranger. You decided youll take an uber for you to get home.

You decided that it was time you finally opened the briefcases. When you opened briefcase 1, you were hit with a vile smell. You quickly throw down the briefcase to see dead rats inside. Disgusted, you wanted to open briefcase 2, to see if something is better in that one. You quickly open the briefcase and see stacks of money inside. You happily close the briefcase and think of all things you can do with the money.

You took your uber and as you approach your apartment building, you reach into your pocket looking for your keys. Frantically, you search your pocket and come to realize that the homeless man stole them. Whomp Whomp, better luck next time!
